MIKE WAZOWSKI

ABOUT

Michael "Mike" Wazowski is one of the two main characters in Monsters, Inc. He is a station runner and coach on the scare floor, and his nickname is Googley Bear.

Mike and his friends

CHARACTERISTICS

  1. He is a short, lime green monster.
  2. He has a single big eyeball and skinny limbs.
  3. He has a goofy, caring and loyal personality.
